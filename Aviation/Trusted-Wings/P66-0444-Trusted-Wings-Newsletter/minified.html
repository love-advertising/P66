Charlie Schouweiler:
Responsible. Trustworthy. Diligent.
 
From run-ins with celebrities as manager at a well-known mortuary and cemetery in Hollywood to off-roading and camping in the Colorado mountains, four-time business owner Charlie Schouweiler has lived life to the fullest. A family man, entrepreneur and adventurer with a 13-year tenure at Phillips 66 as a Supply Analyst, Charlie has maximized Phillips 66® Avgas production while finding happiness in mentoring youth and serving others. His faith, hard work and determination continue to inspire others, and we are honored to recognize his accomplishments in our Employee Spotlight.
